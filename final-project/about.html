About

Ava Clary writes romantic comedies for science fiction and fantasy lovers. She also dabbles in YA fantasy. Faeries, Fae, Faries, fairyland, whatever you want to call them, she puts them in her stories. Clearly, she has a fairy problem herself. Luckily one of her upcoming YA novels will be an epic fantasy with dragons. Her latest novel is Fairy Doctor Returns, a prequel to her romantic fantasy trilogy: The Fairy Doctor.
