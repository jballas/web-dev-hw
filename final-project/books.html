Leda Moser is suffering from heart-breakitis, which makes her rather reckless and insatiably curious. The only cure? An adventure or an extravagantly outrageous affair! Lucky for her, she’s in for both!

A lot has changed since fairy doctor extraordinaire August Aldmire last set foot in London 150 years ago. He’s just escaped from the Tir Na nOg (aka Fairyland). But he’s broke. He’s stranded at Heathrow Airport until he runs into Leda Moser, well, she runs into him first! Then she drags him along in her taxi! And they’re sharing a room too? What next? A kidnapping!?

Leda has no idea about the powerful fae who haunt London’s Underground, in a shadowy world beyond our own. To make matters worse, a monstrous snowstorm has just descended upon the city and she soon finds herself in an adventure the likes of which she’s never seen.

Leda and August’s worlds collide in this charming page-turner from the author of the Fairy Doctor Falls in Love. What are you waiting for? Get yours today!

Ava Clary presents a standalone prequel set 25 years before the original Fairy Doctor trilogy.

Fairies and odd creatures of folklore harass Lillia all the time. She never wanted a career in fairy medicine, but she can't escape them and their silly ailments. He's a rich and powerful magician who can give Lillia everything she wants. Of course, she accepts his proposal. They're headed to an engagement party in Mexico City before the upcoming wedding in glamorous Singapore.

But then Lillia meets Marquez. He's going to be her bodyguard.

Be careful of those hot and sexy Singapore nights.
